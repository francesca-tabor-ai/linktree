## ğŸ¯ Key Design Decisions Explained

### Why No Animations?
Editorial design prioritizes calm and confidence. Your content speaks for itself without needing motion to grab attention.

### Why Left-Aligned?
Creates a document-like, editorial feel. More sophisticated than centered buttons. Similar to Linear and Vercel's approach.

### Why Grouped Sections?
Clear hierarchy helps visitors understand your work at a glance:
- Applied AI systems
- Platform automation
- Product tools
- Prompt engineering
- Governance

### Why Plain Text Contact?
Contact info is reference material, not a CTA. Plain text is:
- Easier to copy
- More accessible
- More professional
- Less pushy

### Why Server Components?
- Faster page loads
- Less JavaScript
- Better SEO
- More performant

Only `LinkItem.tsx` needs client-side code for hover/press interactions.
